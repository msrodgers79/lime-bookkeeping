Bookkeeping

Accurate bookkeeping is the foundation of a well-run business.

I ensure your accounts are:

  • Fully reconciled
  • Correctly coded
  • Structured and organised
  • Ready for VAT, payroll and year-end

This includes bank and credit card reconciliations, income posting (including retention and CIS), and ensuring your records are reliable and consistent month after month.

VAT Returns

I prepare and submit MTD-compliant VAT returns, ensuring:

  • Transactions are reviewed and correctly treated
  • Deadlines are met without last-minute pressure
  • You understand your VAT position in advance

No surprises. No rushed submissions.

CIS Support

For construction industry clients, I provide:

  • Monthly CIS return submissions
  • Subcontractor verification
  • CIS suffered reporting
  • Clear tracking of deductions

CIS compliance requires attention to detail — and I ensure it remains accurate and up to date.

Payroll Services

Professional and confidential payroll support, including:

  • Monthly payroll processing
  • Director payroll
  • RTI submissions
  • Statutory payments
  • Year-end forms

Payroll is handled discreetly and accurately, with clear communication throughout.

Companies House ID Verification

With the introduction of identity verification requirements for company directors and those with significant control, I provide structured support to ensure:

  • Directors are properly verified
  • Companies House compliance is maintained
  • Filings are accurate and timely

This ensures your company records remain up to date and compliant.

Ongoing Financial Oversight

My role is not simply to "process transactions".

I ensure your systems are structured, your responsibilities are clear, and your compliance obligations are met consistently.

When bookkeeping is handled properly, it supports confident business decisions.
