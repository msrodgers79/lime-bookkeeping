About Lindsey

I am a UK-based self-employed bookkeeper, based in Basingstoke, Hampshire, providing structured bookkeeping and compliance support to growing businesses.

Over time, many businesses find that their finances become more complex — funding arrangements, fluctuating payroll, CIS, VAT complications. Without proper structure, this can quickly become stressful.

My approach is calm, methodical and professional.

I believe in

  • Clear processes
  • Defined responsibilities
  • Reliable monthly communication
  • Consistency and accuracy
  • Professional boundaries

I work closely with business owners to ensure their financial records remain organised, compliant and dependable.

My clients value knowing that things are handled properly — without needing to chase, double-check or worry about deadlines.
